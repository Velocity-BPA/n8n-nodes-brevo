# Introducing n8n-nodes-brevo: Supercharge Your Email Marketing Automation

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-brevo, a powerful community node that brings Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) integration directly into your n8n workflows. Created by Velocity BPA, this node makes it easier than ever to automate your email marketing, transactional emails, SMS campaigns, and contact management.

## The Problem We're Solving

Email marketing and transactional email management are critical for modern businesses, but manually syncing data between systems, triggering campaigns, and managing contacts across platforms is time-consuming and error-prone. Many teams struggle to connect their email service provider with their other business tools, leading to disconnected workflows and missed opportunities.

## What is n8n-nodes-brevo?

n8n-nodes-brevo is a comprehensive integration that connects Brevo's robust email and SMS platform with n8n's powerful workflow automation. Whether you're sending transactional emails, managing marketing campaigns, syncing contacts from your CRM, or triggering SMS notifications, this node handles it all within your automated workflows.

## Key Features

Our Brevo node provides complete access to Brevo's API, enabling you to:

- **Manage Contacts**: Create, update, and organize contacts and lists programmatically
- **Send Transactional Emails**: Trigger personalized emails based on user actions or system events
- **Control Campaigns**: Create and manage email marketing campaigns directly from your workflows
- **SMS Integration**: Send SMS messages as part of your multi-channel communication strategy
- **Advanced Segmentation**: Leverage Brevo's powerful contact segmentation within your automation logic

## Getting Started

Installing n8n-nodes-brevo is straightforward. Simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-brevo


After installation, restart your n8n instance, and you'll find the Brevo node available in your node palette. Connect your Brevo API credentials, and you're ready to start automating.
